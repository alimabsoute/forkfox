This is the lead paragraph. Make it strong. State the thesis or biggest finding in the first two sentences. Readers decide whether to continue based on this alone.

Second paragraph provides context. What did we measure? How many dishes? What makes this finding surprising? Write for someone who has never heard of ForkFox — one sentence should explain what the scoring means.

📊
Methodology: We scored X dishes across Y restaurants using the ForkFox algorithm — a patent-pending formula measuring flavor intensity, texture consistency, value, and presentation.

Third paragraph transitions into the first major section. Use it to preview what the reader will learn by the end of the piece.

Section Heading Here

Body paragraph under first heading. Keep paragraphs to 3-5 sentences for readability. Vary sentence length. Short punchy sentences land harder.

Continue the argument. Reference data points, restaurant names, or specific dish details to ground the writing in specifics rather than generalities.

Optional caption. Restaurant name, dish, location, date.

Paragraph after the first image. Reference what was just shown or pivot to the next idea. Images should appear where they're most relevant to the text, not just wherever they fit.

A compelling quote or key finding pulled from the article. Should be the single most shareable line in this section.

Continue after pull quote. The paragraph that follows a pull quote should reinforce or expand on what was quoted — not repeat it.

Second Section Heading

Opening of second major section. If discussing rankings, name names. Readers love specifics: "Terakawa's tonkotsu earned a 91 on flavor alone" beats "several ramen spots scored well."

The Verdict

Closing paragraph. Bring it back to the lead premise. What did we learn? What should the reader do next — visit a restaurant, try a specific dish, download the app? End on something memorable.

Final sentence. Can be a question that opens the next article, a call to action, or a sharp one-liner that lands the thesis one last time.
